Welcome to the Pencil Preschool

At Pencil, we prioritize empowering children to learn at their own pace, driven by their imagination and interests. Our educators foster a carefully crafted, adaptable learning space that caters to each child's unique needs.

Why The Pencil Preschool

Personalized Learning

With an 8:1 student-teacher ratio, we ensure focused, individualized attention that enhances each student’s learning experience.

Exploration

Exploration is at the heart of our curriculum, integrating Montessori, Reggio Emilia, and project-based learning methods. Each student participates in over 25 tailored exploration sessions annually.

A Purposefully Designed Environment

Our activity studios offer 6 dedicated spaces for literacy, art, music, drama, and STEM, fostering creativity and skill development.

Observation-Based, Data-Driven Assessment

Our teachers conduct over 100 personalized observations per year, gaining in-depth insights into each child’s academic and socio-emotional progress
