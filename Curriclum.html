Curriculum & Programs

A curriculum incorporating 32 research-based skills.

Our GEM curriculum system seamlessly integrates 32 research-based skills into playful games and projects, inspired by Finland's Early Education approach. As children engage, they naturally progress in all areas of development—social-emotional, physical, language, and cognitive.

Programs by Age Group

Explore our comprehensive programs, tailored for each age group, designed to nurture and support the unique developmental needs of every child at The Pencil Preschool & Daycare.

Toddler

(1.5 – 3 Years)

In our Toddler Program, we focus on each child's natural growth and development, offering responsive care and enriching sensory learning experiences.

With our toddler curriculum, you’ll:

Offer sensory-rich play opportunities that inspire children to explore their creativity through artistic expression, dramatic play, and problem-solving challenges.

Work closely with families, sharing insights and observations on each child's unique progress and development.

Build nurturing relationships with children, helping them express and understand their emotions.

Nursery

(3 – 4 Years)

In the Nursery program, children begin a journey of self-expression, sharing their thoughts, feelings, and experiences both verbally and through actions. They develop an appreciation for storytelling in various forms—art, speech, drama, role play, songs, and books. As they explore their surroundings and available resources, their understanding of the world expands. This stage also enhances their social skills and problem-solving abilities.

Junior KG

(4 – 5 Years)

In Junior KG, children will have chances to develop leadership abilities as they become more capable of taking responsibility for themselves and their surroundings.

They will begin expressing themselves through writing, using phonics and sight word vocabulary. Engaging in inquiry-based projects that involve research and presentations will spark their curiosity, encouraging them to ask questions, seek answers, and share their understanding.

Senior KG

(5 – 6 Years)

In Senior KG, our curriculum is designed to prepare children for success in primary school and beyond!

Your child will gain a strong foundation in literacy and numeracy, develop critical thinking and reasoning skills, and cultivate a positive mindset—key attributes for thriving as they move into the next stage of their academic journey.

Afterschool Care

Our afterschool care programs offer children of all ages the chance to build new skills, confidence, and a love for learning. From school preparation to extracurricular activities, each program provides personalized instruction in small, supportive classroom settings. With low teacher-to-child ratios, our programs are tailored to meet each child's unique learning style and pace.
